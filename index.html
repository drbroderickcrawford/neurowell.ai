Our Mission

NEUROWELL is dedicated to improving outcomes for patients and families affected by rare neurological diseases, including Creutzfeldt-Jakob Disease (CJD), through cutting-edge research, technology, and compassionate support.

Innovating with AI & Deep Learning

  • Building Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to analyze brain scans for earlier and more accurate CJD detection
  • Creating tools for clinicians and researchers to better understand disease patterns
  • Developing explainable AI to help interpret diagnostic results
  • Supporting patients and caregivers with education and resources
